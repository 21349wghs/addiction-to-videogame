About Gaming Addiction

Understanding Video Game Addiction: Effects, Risks, and Solutions

Video games are a big part of modern life, they can be fun, immersive, and even educational. For many, gaming is a way to relax, sharpen their reflexes, or connect with friends. But like anything enjoyable, too much of it can lead to problems. This page explores the growing concern of video game addiction, especially among teenagers, and how it affects mental, emotional, and physical health.
