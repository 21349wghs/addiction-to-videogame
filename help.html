Need Help with Video Game Addiction?

Find out how to spot the signs, understand the effects, and take steps toward a healthier balance.

Signs of addiction

Video game addiction hurts your life by making you ignore school, friends, and health. It can cause tiredness, poor sleep, pain, and feelings of stress or loneliness.

How gaming affects Health

Playing too much can hurt your mind and body. It may cause anxiety, poor sleep, back pain, and make you less active.

What can you do

Set time limits, take breaks, and avoid gaming before bed. Do other activities and spend time with friends. If gaming causes problems, ask a doctor, or counselor for help. You’re not alone.

Play Smart, Live Better

Think gaming is just harmless fun? It can be until it starts messing with your sleep, stress, focus, and even your health. This section dives into how too much screen time can affect your mental health, brain power, and body, and gives you tips to find balance before the game starts playing you.
