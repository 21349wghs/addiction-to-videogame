Quit Now!

Every hour you spend in a game is an hour you could be building the life you dream about. Imagine if the same energy you put into levelling up your character was spent levelling up yourself, your health, your skills, and your relationships. One day you will not remember the matches you won, but you will feel the life you built because you chose to play the real game. You can do it.

Life moves forward whether you play or not. While a game can be paused, real moments cannot. Friendships, experiences, and opportunities pass by quietly when you are too focused on a screen. Look up, step out, and take part in the world around you because the memories you make outside the game will be the ones you carry forever.
